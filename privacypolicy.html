We collect solely information that is necessary to provide you with our services and to improve our product.

Where We Store Your Data

Usage Statistics

Aggregated and Anonymous Usage information are stored in Amazon Web Services our cloud storage provider.

Diagnostic and Crash Reporting Data.

All those information are sent to Sentry our Crash Reporting tool provider.

For customer support and email services, we use ZOHO. This means that every email you send to us will be stored at ZOHO. Additionally, we are legally required to archive all email traffic for ten years. This is done by our email provider company ZOHO. We do not have direct access to the created backups.

How Long We Store Your Data For

We store your data for as long as Spain law requires, or as is necessary for the fulfillment or the initiation of a contract, or as long as we claim legitimate interests. After the expiration of that period, the corresponding data is routinely deleted or completely anonymized. We have detailed some of the deletion periods under “Where We Store Your Data”. Usage Statistics is generally never deleted. Diagnostic and Crash reporting has a maximum retention of 30 days.

Your Privacy Rights (Under GDPR)

Right to Access. You can request Koriola to provide you with information on how we collect, use, and store your personal information, and to provide you with a copy of your personal information we store.

Right of Rectification. You can request that we correct inaccurate information about you.

Right to Delete. You can request that we delete information collected about you, given that we are not required by law to preserve it, that it is not necessary for contract fulfillment and that we can still identify your records.

Right to Object. You can object to the processing of your information in certain cases, as well as request that Koriola does not use your personal information for direct marketing purposes.

Right to Data Portability. If requested, we will provide you all data under our control in common, machine-readable formats. If requested, we will provide you with instructions to obtain your data, in cases where we do not have direct access.
